About Us

Lakeville Assisted Living is a residential home offering placement for residents interested in a 24-hour assisted living program. We offer several amenities that residents may enjoy and benefit from, including a nearby shopping strip with restaurants, grocery stores, coffee shops, and more. Our experienced staff and registered nurses are dedicated to each resident. We will work with you to set up a tour of the house, assist with moving and settling in, and provide a great living experience.

Mission Statement

Lakeville Assisted Living LLC is dedicated to providing compassionate, person-centered assisted living services that promote dignity, independence, safety, and quality of life for every resident. We strive to create a supportive home-like environment where residents feel respected, valued, and empowered to live their best lives.

Vision Statement

To be a trusted leader in assisted living care within our community by delivering exceptional services, fostering meaningful relationships, and continuously improving the well-being of the individuals we serve.

Core Values

Compassion

We approach every resident and family with empathy, kindness, and genuine care, recognizing each individual's unique needs and life experiences.

Respect

We honor the rights, privacy, cultural backgrounds, and personal choices of all residents, ensuring they are treated with dignity at all times.

Person-Centered Care

We tailor services to meet the physical, emotional, and social needs of each resident, encouraging independence while providing appropriate support.

Integrity

We operate with honesty, transparency, and ethical standards, maintaining compliance with all regulations and holding ourselves accountable for the quality of care we provide.

Collaboration

We value open communication and work closely with residents, families, healthcare providers, and community partners to ensure coordinated and effective care.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of services designed to support each resident's independence, health, and happiness.

Personal Care

Help with Activities of Daily Living (ADLs) such as bathing, dressing, grooming, and mobility — delivered with respect and sensitivity.

Medication Support

Medication administration, management, and daily wellness checks by licensed registered nurses.

Dining & Nutrition

Well-prepared meals and snacks, often served in communal dining spaces, with special diets accommodated.

Housekeeping

Laundry services, light housekeeping, and maintenance so residents can enjoy a clean, comfortable home.

Life Enrichment

Group wellness classes, social gatherings, and recreational activities to foster connection and joy.

Transportation

Scheduled rides for medical appointments, shopping, and local outings so residents stay connected to the community.
